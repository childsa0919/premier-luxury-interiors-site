01 / Kitchens

Kitchens designed for daily life at a higher level.

The kitchen is the most demanding room in the house. It's also the one guests judge first. We build kitchens that handle weeknight dinners and Saturday parties with equal grace — quietly engineered, beautifully finished, and made from materials that look better five years in.

What's included

The full design-build scope.

Every kitchen project is delivered turnkey — one studio, one estimate, one accountable team from concept through final reveal. No managing six trades or chasing punch-list items.

01

Custom Cabinetry

Inset, full-overlay, or shaker — locally built to your dimensions with hand-applied finishes, reeded or paneled doors, and integrated lighting.

02

Natural Stone

Marble, quartzite, soapstone, and onyx — book-matched slabs, mitered waterfalls, and templated on-site for seam-free transitions.

03

Integrated Appliances

Sub-Zero, Wolf, Miele, La Cornue. Panel-ready refrigeration, plaster hoods, induction-or-gas, sommelier wine storage.

04

Layered Lighting

Recessed, decorative, under-cabinet, and toe-kick — all on dimmable scenes, with low-glare optics where it matters.

05

Plumbing & Fittings

Waterworks, Brizo Litze, Perrin & Rowe, and Kallista. Unlacquered brass, brushed nickel, or PVD finishes that age the way you want them to.

06

Pantry & Scullery

Working pantries, hidden butler's spaces, integrated coffee & espresso, prep sinks, and beverage stations engineered into the plan.
