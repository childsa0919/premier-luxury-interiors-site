Privacy
Policy.

Effective July 12, 2026

This policy explains how information is handled when you visit our website, submit a project inquiry, or communicate with our team.

Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, phone number, property location, project interests, desired timing, investment-planning status, communication preferences, and the details you share about your home or project.

When you use the website, we may also receive limited technical and attribution information such as your browser type, device information, referring page, landing page, and campaign parameters. We use this information to operate the site, understand inquiry sources, prevent misuse, and improve our services.

How we use information

We use information to respond to inquiries, evaluate whether a project may be a fit, schedule consultations, communicate about requested services, maintain business records, improve our website and client experience, and protect our systems.

If you separately and optionally consent to text messages, we may use your mobile number for non-marketing communications related to your consultation request, scheduling, and project updates. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP.

Mobile information and SMS consent

Premier Luxury Interiors does not share mobile information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information may be disclosed to service providers that support our operations, such as customer-service and communications providers. Text-message opt-in data and consent are excluded from all other sharing and will not be disclosed to any third party for its own marketing.

SMS consent is optional and is not a condition of purchasing services. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for assistance or STOP to opt out.

How information may be shared

We do not sell personal information. We may provide limited information to vendors that help us host the website, deliver communications, manage inquiries, analyze site performance, or provide other services on our behalf. These providers are permitted to use the information only to support the services they provide to us.

We may also disclose information when reasonably necessary to comply with law, respond to lawful requests, protect rights and safety, investigate fraud or misuse, or support a business transfer subject to appropriate safeguards.

Cookies and analytics

The site may use essential browser storage and limited analytics technologies to remember preferences, measure performance, understand traffic sources, and improve the website. You can manage cookies through your browser settings, although disabling certain technologies may affect site functionality.

Data retention and security

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to inquiries, provide services, meet legal and accounting obligations, resolve disputes, and protect our business. We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information, but no online system can be guaranteed completely secure.

Your choices

You may ask to review, correct, or delete information you have provided, subject to legal and operational retention requirements. You may unsubscribe from email by following the instructions in a message and may opt out of text messages by replying STOP.

Children’s privacy

Our services are intended for adults planning residential remodeling projects. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as our practices or legal requirements change. The effective date above identifies the latest revision.
