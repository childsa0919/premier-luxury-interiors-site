Why design–build

When one studio holds the pencil and the hammer, nothing is lost in translation.

Most renovations pass through many hands — a designer, a showroom, a contractor, their subs — and every handoff costs intent. Premier Luxury Interiors keeps the entire act in one place.

  1. 01

    Design discipline

    Proportion, light, and storage are resolved on paper before anything is demolished. The drawings are the contract with the room.

  2. 02

    Material curation

    Stone, millwork, and metal are chosen in hand — not from a chip. Every surface is selected for how it wears, not just how it photographs.

  3. 03

    Construction execution

    The studio that draws the room builds the room. Tolerances hold because the same eye follows every trade to the last reveal line.

  4. 04

    Accountable stewardship

    One agreement, one schedule, one point of accountability — from the first site visit to the final walkthrough.

What happens next

From a conversation to a finished room.

No pressure, no obligation — a clear sequence you can see all the way to the end.

  1. 01

    Private consultation

    We walk your space together, listen to how you live in it, and speak plainly about scope, timeline, and investment.

  2. 02

    Design & drawings

    The room is resolved on paper — plans, elevations, and details you can read and question before anything is ordered.

  3. 03

    Selections

    Materials chosen in hand — stone slabs, door samples, and finishes considered under your own light.

  4. 04

    Construction

    One team, one schedule. We protect your home, keep the site orderly, and keep you informed at every stage.

  5. 05

    The reveal

    A finished kitchen or bath, walked through together — every detail inspected before we call it done.
