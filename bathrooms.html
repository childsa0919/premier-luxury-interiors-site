02 / Bathrooms

Bathrooms that feel like the quietest room in the house.

Master suites, powder rooms, and wellness baths designed around honed stone, sculptural fixtures, and the small luxuries that turn a morning routine into something worth slowing down for.

What's included

Spa-caliber, thoughtfully engineered.

Bathrooms are the room where small details get noticed most. Tile reveals, drain placement, niche dimensions, even the way the door swings — we sweat all of it.

01

Honed Stone

Floor-to-ceiling stone slabs, book-matched accent walls, or hand-set zellige and mosaic — sourced from yards we know personally.

02

Sculptural Tubs

Apaiser, Victoria + Albert, Kaldewei — freestanding or undermount, with cast stone, copper, or honed limestone options.

03

Custom Vanities

Rift-cut oak, fluted walnut, or lacquered cabinetry with integrated stone sinks and quiet-close everything.

04

Wet Rooms & Steam

Curbless showers, linear drains, ThermaSol steam, Mr. Steam aromatherapy, and chromatherapy lighting.

05

Radiant Floors

Hydronic or electric radiant heating, thin-set under stone, on programmable scenes so the floor is warm before you are.

06

Layered Lighting

Recessed, sconce, and cove lighting on dimmable scenes — flattering glow at the mirror, gentle path lighting at night.

A specialty

Powder rooms that steal the show.

The half-bath off the entry is the smallest room in the house and often the most memorable. We treat it like a jewel box — dramatic wallcoverings, unusual stone, lighting that flatters everyone who walks in.

Powder rooms are also our favorite way for clients to try working with us before committing to a larger project.
